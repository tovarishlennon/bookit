F.A.Q

1. I bought a ticket, what should I do next?

You will receive a ticket in your personal account or by mail. You can save it or print it out if desired. Come to the event 30 minutes before it starts and exchange the electronic version of the ticket at the box office. To do this, you need to provide a QR code for scanning or call the confirmation number to the cashier.

2. I have lost my ticket or do not have access to it. Can you send it to me again?

Yes, to do this, call our hotline at +998(71)333-33-33 or in your application via chat. You can restore your ticket by confirming one of the following: the last 4 digits of your bank card, the phone number registered in the system, your ID number in the system or your mailing address.

3. I am in the cinema, and the cashier tells me that my ticket number is not registered in the system. What do i do?

Carefully check the information in your electronic ticket: number, session, cinema, city. If everything is correct, but the cashier claims that there is no such ticket number in their system, ask the administrator to contact us on the contact numbers or call us: +998(71)333-33-33

4. I do not fit under the age limit, will they let me through?

Only the cinema to which you are going to go can answer this question. By law, the cinema has every right to keep you out of the show.

5. The money from the card was written off, but the ticket number did not come in the mail. What to do?

Make sure you did everything right. Check your mail and update the “tickets” section. If you did not find your ticket, please contact our support team by phone number: +998(71)333-33-33 or email us through the application
