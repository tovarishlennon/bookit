The Heart of a Dog

Author: Mikhail Bulgakov

Production: Artem Kim

Starring: Sanjar Jumaev, Bernard Nazarmuhamedov and Yang Dobrynin, Vladimir Yudin, Galina Borisova, Anastasia Pryadkin, Clara nafikova, Alina Zimerman, Julius Placidus, Olga Volodina, Hope Bandelet.

Duration: 120 min

Today, 90 years after the creation of the"The Heart of a Dog", the issues of violence against the person and suppression of human nature have only become more urgent. As the monstrous homunculus Poligraph Poligraphovich Sharikov replaces the Ball-dog, so the modern man loses the last pieces of his personality.

Television, businessmen, politicians and sects fill our minds with their content, creating our desires and habits. Independent choice has become an illusion for the majority. New demiurges and creators create their Adam and Eve, ideal to sell them forbidden apples. Not chloroform the Transfiguration Professor, but the world Network of head intoxicating and creates a new Ball. The devastation in the minds is progressing, the cold Blizzard of alienation has spun people, and the Shvonders are in a hurry to take power into their own hands.

Who today can call himself a completely free man?Source
